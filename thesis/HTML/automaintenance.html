VEHICLE MAINTENANCE SERVICES AT BIONICWHEELS


Car maintenance is always easier when you trust the technician under the hood. Well, we make it our business to know our customers and their cars, and we treat them like we would our own friends and our own vehicles. Come in for any of the auto maintenance services you see below and get quick, convenient service performed by seriously friendly technicians, at some seriously friendly prices.

OIL CHANGE


If you want to give your car the longest life possible, regular oil changes are a must. Motor oil keeps the engine lubricated and working at maximum efficiency, while decreasing the intense heat generated as your car runs. Check your owner’s manual to see how often you should get your oil changed or consult with a technician. If you don’t remember when your last oil change was, it’s better to be safe than sorry! Make an appointment at Bionicwheels for an affordable oil change today.

TUNE UP SERVICE


Your vehicle’s engine consists of hundreds of complex components. To ensure your engine is running most efficiently and effectively, these parts need to be in good working order. Check your owner’s manual to see the recommended tune up schedule. If you’re not getting your usual gas mileage, experiencing noticeable power loss, or experiencing other strange symptoms, it’s definitely time for a tune up.

A/C SERVICE


You shouldn’t be sweating bullets driving from Point A to Point B. Our A/C performance check service includes a visual inspection, performance test, system state of charge test, system control test, and performance leak test. Let the experts at Bionicwheels ensure your ride is a cool, comfortable one!

RADIATOR SERVICE


Did you know that the combustion chamber inside your vehicle’s engine can reach 4,500 degrees Fahrenheit? That’s HOT. Your radiator is in charge of cooling down your engine so that things don’t overheat and cause an engine breakdown. Because the radiator has such an important job, it’s equally important to get it serviced regularly! Consult your owner’s manual to see the recommended time or mileage intervals between radiator services.

VEHICLE INSPECTION


Peace of mind is priceless, and our vehicle inspections offer just that. With every visit to Bioniwheels, you’ll get a free vehicle inspection that looks at your tires, suspension, lights, fluid levels, wipers, belts, battery, hoses, and more. If your car needs more attention, a Complete Vehicle Inspection can be done. By getting regular inspections, you can stop a problem before it starts!
